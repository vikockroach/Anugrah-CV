About

I have two main aims in life:

  1. Honing as many skills I want as to become king of all trades
  2. Create things that contribute to solving social problems in Indonesia

There’s this assumption that you have to focus on something that is linear to your ‘path’. But what is a path other than a different way of saying a branch. The consequence is that I’m hungry to learn. And it shaped my mindset to regard almost everything has a core and connections, that it is T-shaped and people are collaborative in nature.

This kind of mentality drove me to discover things:

  • From qualitative research to creative.
  • From desk researching to cooking.
  • From writing to photography.

But the most important thing, unfamiliar territory doesn’t frighten me. During my college days, it was leading an organization and publishing, presenting research in front of international audiences. As a Community Manager it was social media alongside clear, concise, and impactful writing, project management, and creative. As a business owner it was cooking, marketing and advertising, and photography and videography.

But learning needs a rigorous training and supportive environment and your company is an agile and fast-paced company that can be the right place for me to grow and contribute. Let’s nurture each other!
